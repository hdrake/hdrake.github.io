Outreach

I founded Climate Gamers, an online community of climate scientists and gamers passionate about climate science and climate action. Our primary product is a livestream at twitch.tv/ClimateGamers where we conduct weekly literature reviews of peer-reviewed climate science articles and play video games related to climate change. Check out our channel below and engage in the conversation by asking questions in the chat box!
