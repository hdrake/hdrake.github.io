Outdoors

Art

I am an avid fan of poet Gary Snyder, print-maker Tom Killion, and film director Hayao Miyazaki. These three japanese (or "faux-japanese") artists represent oceans, winds, and mountains with a level of accuracy that scientists can only dream of.
